Francesco Spelta built his reputation over 38 years as a private chef aboard the world's most distinguished luxury yachts. Raised in Pavia — where his grandmother's ceremonial approach to food first shaped his understanding of craft — he carried that philosophy to the world's most discerning tables.

Word passed quietly among ultra-high-net-worth clients long before it became a formal product. That origin defines everything about the brand: Francesco Spelta is not sold. It is earned.

The Art
of Three Days

Norwegian Origin

AAA+ Norwegian salmon, EU CEE approved. Sourced from certified farms in the coldest, cleanest waters. Arrives in Italy within 48 hours of harvest.

Each fillet is prepared and marinated by hand using Francesco's private formula. No automation. No standardisation. Every piece treated individually.

03
